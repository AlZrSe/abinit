1. Introduction.

ABINIT is tested at each merge in the trunk, on a dedicated test farm, see http://dev.abinit.org/environment/buildbot/buildbot-slave-matrix and http://dev.abinit.org/environment/buildbot/buildbot-builder-matrix. The numerous tests contained in ~abinit/tests/doc are executed, and compared with reference files.
