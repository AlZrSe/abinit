2- Wavelets

This file explains how to perform a calculation on a wavelet basis with the ABINIT package.

The use of wavelets (instead of plane waves) is available in ABINIT. At present, it works for the SCF cycles : one can get density, total energy and forces, in some basic cases (HGH or GTH pseudopotentials only, or PAW - in v7.2). Available also : the finite size corrections to the total energy, restart on wavefunctions following the ETSF norm and geometry relaxation using BFGS. Molecular dynamic is also available for test purposes.
